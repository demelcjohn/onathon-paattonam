Onam ponnonam 

        Onam ponnonam poomala pongum
        Puzhayoram painkili paadunnu unarunaroo (onam)
        Ullil njaan pottiya pazhayoru villinte apashruthiyodee
        Paanan korthidunnu pazhaya sheelin izhakal (onam)
        
        Nin thaalam thaalolicha pooncholakal
        Nin kulire poovaay choodiya poonkaavukal
        Thirayukayaayi ninne karayukayaanallo njaan
        Kariyukayaanennil nee natta
        Poo thumba kandangal veendum (onam)
        
        Nin kannil deepam thediya ponnambalam
        Oli kaanaathirulil kezhum ninnambalam
        Thirayukayaayi ninne irula moodiyenne
        Pozhiyukayaanennil naambitta
        Malar dheepa mukulangal veendum (onam)
